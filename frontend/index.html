Frequently Asked Questions
▶How do I write a full post?
Click "Write Post" in the sidebar, type your topic, select Tweet/Thread/LinkedIn format, then click Write Now. You can also click "Write Thread" or "Write Post" buttons directly on any trend card.
▶What do the tags do?
Every tag on a trend card is clickable. Clicking it filters the Trends page to show only narratives with that tag. You can clear the filter anytime with the Clear button.
▶How often does data update?
Data updates every time you click Scan Now. Results cache for 5 minutes. Auto-refresh runs every 5 minutes in the background.
▶What do signal scores mean?
Scores range 0–100. Above 85 means very strong signal. 70–84 is solid emerging trend. Below 70 is early-stage. Focus on surging or breaking momentum badges for the freshest opportunities.
▶Can I install this as a mobile app?
Yes! On Android open Chrome and tap Add to Home Screen. On iPhone open Safari, tap Share, then Add to Home Screen. It installs like a native app.
▶Is this free?
Yes, completely free. Runs on Groq's free AI tier and free hosting. No subscription or credit card required.
